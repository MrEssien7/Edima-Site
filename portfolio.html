Mobile Banking App Redesign

The challenge: Customers were struggling with a dated mobile banking app that made routine transactions cumbersome.

My role: Lead UI/UX designer responsible for research, wireframing, prototyping, and testing.

What I did:

  • Interviewed users and stakeholders to understand pain points and define success metrics.
  • Designed a simplified navigation structure and cleaner visual language.

Result: A streamlined app that improved task completion rates by 30% and reduced support calls.

My role: UI/UX designer supporting a cross‑functional team.
