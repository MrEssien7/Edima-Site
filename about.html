About Me

I’m a UI/UX designer who enjoys turning complex problems into intuitive digital experiences. My work is informed by user research and driven by a love of aesthetics inspired by the classical elegance and natural beauty of William Kent’s designs. When I’m not designing, you can find me exploring Toronto’s art galleries or sketching in the park.
